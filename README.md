# Job Applicants Monthly Analysis

This project analyzes job applicants data over one year
to identify monthly patterns in recruitment activity.

## Dataset
- Columns: ID, Date, Job Title, Job Location, Hired, Easy Apply, Month
- Records: 3000+

## Analysis
- Monthly count of applicants
- Average, maximum and minimum values by month and total number of applications

## Key Insights
Job applications increase steadily from February to July, reaching a peak in July (3,138 applications), which represents the highest recruitment activity of the year.

- The lowest number of applications occurs in February (2,312), suggesting a slowdown in hiring activity early in the year.

- From May to September, monthly applications remain consistently above the annual average (2,716), indicating a sustained high-demand hiring period.

- After July, application volume gradually declines toward the end of the year, reflecting a seasonal slowdown in recruitment cycles.

- Despite seasonal fluctuations, monthly application counts remain within a relatively narrow range (min: 2,312, max: 3,138), suggesting stable overall demand throughout the year.

## Limitations
- Descriptive analysis only
